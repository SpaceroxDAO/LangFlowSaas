ACME Corporation Employee Handbook
Version 3.1 - Updated January 2026

===================================
SECTION 1: VACATION POLICY
===================================

All full-time employees receive paid vacation days as follows:
- Year 1-2: 15 vacation days per year
- Year 3-5: 20 vacation days per year
- Year 6+: 25 vacation days per year

Vacation days must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal.
Unused vacation days may be carried over to the next year (maximum 5 days).

===================================
SECTION 2: SICK LEAVE
===================================

Employees receive 10 sick days per calendar year.
Doctor's note required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.
Sick leave does not carry over to the next year.

===================================
SECTION 3: REMOTE WORK POLICY
===================================

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Remote work days must be scheduled in advance via the team calendar.
Employees must be available during core hours: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM local time.

===================================
SECTION 4: DRESS CODE
===================================

Business casual attire is required Monday through Thursday.
Casual Friday is observed - jeans and sneakers are permitted.
Client-facing meetings require business professional attire.

===================================
SECTION 5: PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
===================================

Annual performance reviews are conducted in December.
Mid-year check-ins occur in June.
Performance ratings: Exceeds Expectations, Meets Expectations, Needs Improvement.

===================================
SECTION 6: EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
===================================

Business expenses must be submitted within 30 days of purchase.
Receipts required for expenses over $25.
Travel expenses must be pre-approved by management.
Maximum meal reimbursement: $50 per day for domestic travel.

===================================
SECTION 7: COMPANY HOLIDAYS
===================================

ACME Corporation observes the following paid holidays:
- New Year's Day (January 1)
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Presidents' Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day (July 4)
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving Day
- Day after Thanksgiving
- Christmas Eve (December 24)
- Christmas Day (December 25)
