## Managing Your Subscription

### Upgrading

1. Go to **Dashboard → Billing**
2. Click **"Upgrade Plan"**
3. Select your new plan
4. Enter payment information
5. Confirm

Your new limits apply immediately!

### Downgrading

1. Go to **Dashboard → Billing**
2. Click **"Change Plan"**
3. Select a lower tier
4. Confirm

Changes take effect at the end of your billing period. Make sure you're within the new plan's limits!

### Canceling

1. Go to **Dashboard → Billing**
2. Click **"Cancel Subscription"**
3. Confirm cancellation

Your account reverts to the Free plan at the end of the billing period. Data is retained for 30 days.

## Usage & Limits

### Monitoring Usage

View your current usage anytime:

1. Go to **Dashboard → Billing**
2. See the **"Usage"** section
3. Track messages, agents, and storage

### What Counts as a Message?

A "message" is counted when:
- A user sends a message to Charlie
- Charlie responds

So a simple back-and-forth = 2 messages.

**Not counted:**
- System messages
- Error messages
- Empty messages

### Approaching Limits

When you reach 80% of your limit:
- You'll receive an email notification
- A banner appears in the dashboard

When you reach 100%:
- New messages return a friendly "limit reached" response
- Existing features continue to work
- Upgrade anytime to restore full access

### Limit Resets

Limits reset monthly on your billing date.

**Example:**
- Billing date: 15th
- On March 15, your message count resets
- You have until April 14 to use your monthly allocation

## Team Management (Team Plan)

### Adding Team Members

1. Go to **Dashboard → Settings → Team**
2. Click **"Invite Member"**
3. Enter their email
4. Select role (Admin, Editor, Viewer)
5. Send invite

### Team Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| Admin | Full access, manage team, billing |
| Editor | Create/edit agents, can't manage team |
| Viewer | View only, can test in playground |

### Removing Team Members

1. Go to **Dashboard → Settings → Team**

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I try Pro features before upgrading?

Yes! Start a 14-day free trial of Pro:
1. Go to Billing
2. Click "Start Pro Trial"
3. No credit card required

### What happens to my data if I downgrade?

- Agents beyond your new limit become "archived"
- Data is kept but not accessible
- Upgrade again to restore access
- After 90 days, archived data may be deleted

### Do you offer refunds?

- Monthly plans: No refunds, but cancel anytime
- Annual plans: Pro-rated refund within 30 days

### Are there discounts for nonprofits?

Yes! Nonprofits get 50% off any plan.
- Email billing@teachcharlie.ai

### Is there a free trial for Team plan?

Yes, 14-day free trial available. Contact sales for extended trials.
